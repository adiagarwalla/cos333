How does it Work?



Ask

Type the skill you want to learn. You can also type the name of the instructor.

Choose

Select the instructor that matches your question.

Learn

Have a live one on one FaceTime like chat with the instructor and get a personalized response to your question


Features




Authentication

You can create your own unique account on Qurious and after logging in once, you do not need to log in ever again unless you explicitly sign out.


Search by skill / name of instructor

Get your question answered promptly by searching for the skill related to the query or by directly typing in the name of the instructor.


Notifications

Receive a push notification, whether the app is in the foreground, background or even closed when someone wants to have a session with you. Directly enter into the app by clicking on the push notification. Also, deleting notifications triggers another notification to the person who requested the session, so that he does not wait for you to join. Notifications expire after approximately 1 minute.


Profile

Each user has his / her own profile where the name, contact details and a short bio can be edited. A picture can also be uploaded for each user. Also, each user has a list of skills by which he / she is searched which can be added, deleted and edited. Editing skill is optimized for future payment and user rating implementation.


Sessions

FaceTime like video chat interface enabled by OpenTok.
